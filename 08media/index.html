Fun Facts About Cats

  • The oldest known pet cat existed 9,500 years ago. In 2004, archaeologists found a cat grave in Cyprus, which is 9,500 years old, predating Egyptian art by over 4,000 years!
  • Cats spend 70% of their lives sleeping, which is about 13-16 hours a day.
  • A cat named Stubbs was the mayor of Talkeetna, Alaska, for 20 years!
  • The longest cat on record was a Maine Coon named Stewie, measuring 48.5 inches.
  • The richest cat in the world, Blackie, inherited 7 million pounds when its owner passed away!
  • Cats walk like camels and giraffes, moving both right feet first, followed by both left feet.
  • Isaac Newton is said to have invented the cat door to prevent his cats from interrupting his experiments.
  • In 1963, a cat named Felicette (aka "Astrocat") became the first and only cat to go to space.
  • In Ancient Egypt, people would shave off their eyebrows to mourn their pet cat's death.
  • House cats share 95.6% of their genetic makeup with tigers, and they even have similar behaviors like stalking and pouncing!
