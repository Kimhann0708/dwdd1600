About Us

Welcome to Cat Gallery Capture, the ultimate destination for cat lovers to celebrate their favorite feline friends! Our mission is to provide a fun and creative space where people can come together to share adorable, funny, and artistic cat content.

Our Vision:

To become the go-to destination for cat lovers everywhere, providing a place to share and explore the beauty and charm of cats.

Our Community:

We're more than just a gallery. We are a community. We encourage users to contribute their own cat photos and interact with other cat lovers.

Our History:

Cat Gallery Capture began in 2024 as a small passion project by a group of cat enthusiasts who wanted to create a unique online space dedicated to celebrating cats in all their forms.

Fun Fact

If you thought cats spent a lot of their lives sleeping, you’d be right. According to Veterinary Hub, cats actually spend 70% of their lives sleeping, which works out to around 13-16 hours a day. It’s a cat’s life!
